Project DON

Diversity • Opportunity • Novelty



Malaysia first talent and modelling agency that works with and casts Differently-Abled and Unique models. A brand new talent & modelling agency where we will be working with and showcasing so many amazing individuals who are all exquisitely unique / differently-abled in their own way.

DON stands for
Diversity • Opportunity • Novelty

Our tagline for Project DON is 'Beyond Labels'. For instance, DON is the label that goes beyond the 'labels' that confine, restrict and define us.

DON's value proposition

We believe in Diversity, we create Opportunities, and we are not afraid of pushing new ways of doing things, we are Novel. We are celebrating the uniqueness and strength from each one of our members!
