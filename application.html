Apply To Be A Model

We accept applications from anyone with physical or mental limitations regardless of age. Before applying, there are a few things that needs to be taken under consideration such as travelling at short notice, taking directions from others, flexible schedules, etc.

Please send 4 or 5 photos (close-up, half body, and full body shot) and the following details;
• Full name
• Date of Birth
• Diagnosis
• Current location
• Experience or Achievements
• Talents
• Phone Number

It is important to fulfill the above requirements before emailing your application. Please note that we cannot guarantee who will be selected by our clients; however, we will try our best to find you bookings for modelling.

If you have any questions, feel free to email us.
